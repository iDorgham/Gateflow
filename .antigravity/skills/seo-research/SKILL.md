# SEO Research

## Keyword Research

1. **Seed terms** — Product, features, problem/solution
2. **Volume & difficulty** — Use tools (Ahrefs, SEMrush, Google Keyword Planner)
3. **Intent** — Informational, transactional, navigational

## Competitor Analysis

- Top-ranking pages for target keywords
- Content gaps (what competitors have that you don't)
- Backlink profile (quantity/quality)

## Search Intent

| Intent | Content type |
|--------|--------------|
| Informational | Guide, how-to, definition |
| Transactional | Product page, pricing, CTA |
| Navigational | Brand/feature page |

## Output

- Keyword list with volume, difficulty, intent
- Competitor URLs and content summary
- Recommended content topics
