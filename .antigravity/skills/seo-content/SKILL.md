# SEO Content

## Structure

1. **Title (H1)** — Primary keyword near front, 50–60 chars
2. **Meta description** — 150–160 chars, CTA, primary keyword
3. **First 100 words** — Keyword in first paragraph, hook reader
4. **Headings** — H2/H3 with secondary keywords
5. **Body** — 300+ words for substantive pages; answer search intent

## Writing

- **Keyword density** — Natural placement; avoid stuffing
- **Readability** — Short sentences, subheadings, bullets
- **E-E-A-T** — Experience, expertise, authority, trust (credible sources, author)

## Technical

- Unique title and meta per page
- Canonical URLs
- Schema markup where appropriate (Article, Product, FAQ)

## i18n

For GateFlow: Use `t('key', 'fallback')` for user-facing text. Include AR/EN variants for MENA.
